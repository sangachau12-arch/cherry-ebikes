Battery Diagnostics & Repair

eBike Battery Diagnostics, Rebuilds & Replacements

Professional battery diagnostics and repair in Toronto. We test with real data — not just a voltage check.

What We Test

Real data. Real decisions.

Most eBike battery problems aren't obvious from the outside. A voltage check alone doesn't tell you whether your battery will deliver under load, how much real capacity remains, or whether it's safe. We measure all three.

Capacity Remaining

How much usable energy the battery can still store vs. new. The most important number for real-world range.

Voltage Stability Under Load

How well the battery holds voltage during acceleration and hills. Instability means power cutouts and safety risk.

Internal Resistance

How much energy is lost as heat. High resistance causes poor performance, stress on the controller and motor.

Services Available

Battery Rebuild

Technical Methodology

Battery Health Report™ — Technical Methodology

For customers who want deeper data — test methodology, load profiles, measurement limits, and interpretation notes.

Test Methodology

How we measure battery condition

Capacity Testing Protocol
Full charge/discharge cycle under controlled conditions to measure usable amp-hours versus the rated specification. Results normalized to current BMS parameters.
Load Profile Testing
Simulated load profiles approximating real-world riding conditions — flat urban, uphill stress, and sustained discharge — to measure voltage stability under varying demand.
Internal Resistance Measurement
AC impedance measurement at multiple state-of-charge levels. Results compared against baseline values for cell chemistry and configuration to generate a relative resistance rating.
Measurement Limits & Notes
Results Reflect Current Condition

The report documents battery condition at the time of testing. Subsequent use, charging patterns, and storage conditions will affect future performance.

BMS Limitations

Battery Management Systems on some brands restrict access to cell-level data. Where BMS data is limited, measurements are based on system-level behavior under load.

Not a Safety Certification

The Cherry Battery Health Report is a performance assessment, not a safety certification or warranty. It provides performance data for informed decision-making.

Proprietary vs. Standard Batteries

Testing methodology is adapted based on battery architecture. Proprietary systems may have limited data access; this is noted in the report when applicable.

CASE

Canadian Authority for Small Electric Vehicles

A public safety and education initiative focused on raising awareness, standards, and accountability in the rapidly growing eBike and eScooter industry.

What Is CASE?

Education, not marketing.

CASE exists to provide clear, practical education about small electric vehicles. As eBikes and eScooters become more common in cities, condos, and workplaces, battery safety, maintenance responsibility, and informed ownership have never been more important.

❌ This is not a sales presentation. Not a repair or upsell session. Not technician training or recruitment. This session is education only — focused on safety, awareness, and informed decision-making.
